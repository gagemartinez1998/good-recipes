Baja Shrimp Tacos

Description

This delicious recipe is a fusion of tropical flavor and mexicana cuisine. It results in a fantastically fresh dish bursting with flavor.

This dish is easy to make, requiring readily available ingredients found at any local supermarket, and can be ready within an hour. A favorite at parties, family gatherings, or even a simple night with the family.

Ingredients

  • Almond Flour
  • Shrimp Seasoning
  • Plain Greek Yogurt
  • Vinegar
  • Lime Juice
  • Honey
  • Sauce Seasoning
  • Shredded Cabbage and Carrots
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Taco Toppings
